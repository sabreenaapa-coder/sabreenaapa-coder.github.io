    Why CastAdhan exists

    The adhan was never meant to belong to one person’s phone.

    For centuries it called families. Neighbours. Communities.

    It travelled through streets, over rooftops, through walls, into homes and hearts — gently reminding people to return to the remembrance of Allah.

    Today, the adhan often comes from a phone left face-down, on silent, in another room.

    CastAdhan brings that shared experience back into the home.
